Lipid + Milk Cleansing Bar — Bare

⚿ Locked
INCI String
Ingredients: Sodium Tallowate, Sodium Olivate, Sodium Cocoate, Sodium Castorate, Capra Hircus (Goat) Milk, Avena Sativa (Oat) Kernel Flour, Mel (Honey), Glycerin.
Allergen statements required on label: Contains goat milk. Contains honey.
Formula — Oil Phase
Ingredient% of oil weightNotes
Tallow (grass-fed) → Sodium Tallowate45.7%Hard bar · rich lather · barrier lipids
Olive oil pomace → Sodium Olivate28.6%Conditioning · classic soapmaking oil
Coconut oil 76° refined → Sodium Cocoate11.4%Lather · cleansing
Mango seed butter → (in Sodium Olivate/Tallowate)8.6%Conditioning · emollient
Castor oil → Sodium Castorate5.7%Lather boost · conditioning
Process Notes
Cold process. Superfat: 7%. Liquid: frozen goat milk at 38% of oil weight — freeze solid before adding lye.
Additives: Colloidal oat 1 tsp per lb of oils · Raw honey 1 tsp per lb of oils (add at light trace).
Cure: 42 days minimum. Lye: NaOH (sodium hydroxide) 100% pure. Use lye calculator before every batch.
